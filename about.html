About

Who We Are

Palaiseau Sydney Club is a football club created to bring together players, supporters, and the local community through a shared passion for sport.

The club identity reflects unity, progress, and ambition. Its slogan, Always Advancing, represents the desire to improve both on and off the field.

The club also celebrates a connection between Palaiseau and Sydney, combining local roots with an international sporting vision.
