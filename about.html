About

Who We Are

Palaiseau Sydney Club is a football club built from the passion of two cities with the ambition to achieve great things. The club aims to bring together players and supporters around a shared passion: football.

Our club is committed to improving both on and off the pitch.

Thanks to the support of its economic partners, but also to the enthusiasm of the supporters, PSC has created a strong sporting environment where players and staff can develop, perform, and represent the club with pride and love.

Club Info
