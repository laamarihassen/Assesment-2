About

Who We Are

Palaiseau Sydney Club is a football club built around passion, ambition, and team spirit. The club brings together players, supporters, and the local community through a shared love of the game.

Our identity is based on progress, discipline, and unity. The slogan “Always Advancing” reflects the club’s commitment to improving on and off the pitch.

Through training, competition, and community involvement, the club aims to create a strong sporting environment where players can grow, perform, and represent the club with pride.

Club Info

Location: Palaiseau, France
